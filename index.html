About

Hello, my name is Aspera Astra and I am an intuitive healer. My name means "through hardship to the stars," and it reflects my belief that our deepest wounds often hold the keys to our greatest transformation. I work as a conduit, not a director: holding space for what wants to emerge in you.

Two years ago, during an Ayahuasca ceremony, I experienced a profound spiritual awakening that changed the course of my life. During that experience, I made contact with spirit guides who called me to a path of service. What began as a personal transformation evolved into a deep connection with the spirit world and a commitment to helping others.

Since then, I have dedicated myself to developing this connection through meditation, spiritual practice, and direct work with my guides. Today, I offer intuitive readings and spiritual guidance to help people navigate life's challenges, transitions, relationships, and questions of purpose.

My practice weaves intuitive perception with energetic healing, honoring both the seen and unseen dimensions of your experience. Every session is unique, guided by what your spirit is ready to receive. I approach every session with compassion, honesty, and respect. My role is not to tell you what to believe or what decisions to make, but to share the messages, insights, and perspectives that come through so that you can move forward with greater clarity and confidence.

Thank you for your trust and support. I am honored to be part of your journey.

"The answers you seek are already within you, sometimes you just need someone to help you hear them."
